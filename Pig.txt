The pig was the first mob in bloxd.io

Overview
Type: Passive Mob
Drops: Raw Porkchop
Spawn Biomes: Forests (rare), Plains (fairly rare), Meadows (not so rare)

Appearance
Pigs are pink, grunting creatures resembling their real-life counterparts, often moving in small herds.

Obtaining
Natural Generation: Pigs spawn naturally in specific biomes such as Forests, Plains, and Meadows.
Breeding: Currently not available.

Uses
Food: Pigs drop Raw Porkchops, which players can cook for sustenance.
